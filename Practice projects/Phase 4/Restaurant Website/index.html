Explore Menu

Chilaquiles

Made of triangular pieces of fried or toasted corn tortilla, called totopos, soaked in a red or green hot sauce, they are topped with either shredded chicken, chorizo, shredded beef, and scrambled eggs or a sunny side-up egg.

Machaca

Dried red meat such as beef or pork mixed with spicy peppers, tomato and onion.

Tacos

Taco fillings are beef steak, flank steak, chorizo, offal, “al pastor”, as well as hot and sweet marinated pork.

Burritos

A burrito is a cylindrically rolled flour tortilla stuffed with different ingredients of choice, often a stew.

Discada

Discada is popular northern Mexican dish that is a carnival for carnivores: sausages, chorizo, ground meat, ham, bacon, and lard – all these go in a good discada.

Huevos Rancheros

It´s made with two fried corn tortillas, topped with fried beans, and two sunny side up eggs all bathed in red hot sauce and decorated with coriander and freshly ground black pepper. Simply delicious!

Quesadilla

Blue corn quesadilla, filled with Cochinita Pibil and, of course, cheese.

Menudo

Menudo is a seasoned soup made with hominy (dried corn kernels), onions, oregano and chilies, and of course, beef tripe.

Cochinita Pibil

The pork meat is marinated in achiote, orange juice, onion, and vinegar, then cooked wrapped in banana leaves.
